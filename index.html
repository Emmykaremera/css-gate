What is FeWo-direkt?

FeWo-direkt is your competent partner for vacations in a holiday home or apartment. As part of Vrbo, one of the leading online marketplaces for holiday home rentals, FeWo-direkt offers you more than two million unique accommodations, including fincas, bungalows, apartments, holiday cabins, and even villas. Secure payments, 24/7 support, and the Book with Confidence Guarantee make booking a breeze. The user-friendly website and numerous practical filters allow you to find your dream vacation apartment for your budget with little effort. This way, you are sure to spend an unforgettable vacation that is completely stress-free from the search and booking to the stay and departure.

Renting a holiday apartment or holiday home: These are the advantages

Families and groups in particular appreciate the advantages of holiday accommodation: more space, more privacy, more amenities. You have more flexibility and freedom in your own four walls than in conventional hotels and can plan your vacation freely - regardless of whether you want to go on a city trip or unwind with your dog in the middle of picturesque nature. Are you looking for a cozy holiday cabin in the mountains with a washing machine and whirlpool? A beach house on the water or a large finca in the south with a pool and fully equipped kitchen? No problem, because holiday accommodations are available in all sizes and with every desired feature that you have in mind for your next family vacation, adventure trip with friends or a romantic weekend.
